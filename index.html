Auckland Underwater Rugby

New Zealand’s best competitive Underwater Rugby team

We train hard, play harder, and always look out for each other. Underwater Rugby is more than a sport — it’s a challenge, a community, and a way to grow stronger together. Whether you want to compete internationally or just try something new, there’s space for you in the Auckland Underwater Rugby family.

The world's only 3D team sport

Underwater Rugby is a fast-paced, full-contact sport played in three dimensions — under the surface, around the goal, and through your limits. Two teams of six battle it out at the bottom of a deep pool, using breath control, strategy, and trust to score in weighted goals. It's intense, it's global, and it's unlike anything you've ever played.

About Us

How It Started

Founded in Auckland, AUWR began as a small group of passionate players wanting to bring something different to the water. Over the years, we've grown into a diverse, international community of athletes who meet weekly to train, compete, and have fun.

"Breath In, Breath Out. Let your body do what your body is capable of doing"

- Wim Hof (Indirect Team Breathing Coach)

What We're About

We train twice a week, participate in national and international tournaments, and welcome players of all levels. Whether you're a total beginner or an experienced athlete, you'll find support, laughs, and serious gameplay in our sessions.

The AUWR Spirit

We take our game seriously — but never ourselves. Expect late-night memes, sideline banter, and shared rides to comps. We're here to win, but more importantly, to grow as a team and as people.
